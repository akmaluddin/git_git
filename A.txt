Marigold's wing began to heal under Alfred's meticulous attention. The dull browns and grays of her feathers started to give way to vibrant yellows and oranges, mirroring the warmth that had entered Alfred's life. He constructed a makeshift cage from an old birdhouse and some chicken wire, placing it near the window so she could watch the world outside. Each day, Alfred would sit beside her, sharing tales of his past and the adventures he had once dreamed of.
