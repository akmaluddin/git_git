With trembling hands, he fetched a small box from the cupboard above the stove. Inside lay a set of tweezers and a bottle of antiseptic that had been there since his wife's passing. He hadn't used them in years, but they were still in pristine condition. He approached the bird cautiously, whispering soothing words that he hoped would ease its distress. The bird's eyes, though filled with pain, held a spark of trust that Alfred found both humbling and heart-wrenching.
